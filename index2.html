About Me

I'm an 11th-grade student at Jamnabai Narsee School, passionate about aerospace engineering, astrophysics, and space exploration.

I am currently conducting research on **Sustainable Aviation Fuels (SAF)** and working on a research paper in this field. My interests include propulsion systems, orbital mechanics, and next-generation space travel technologies.

Beyond research, I have completed **internships at the Nehru Science Centre and Nehru Planetarium**, gaining hands-on exposure to real-world scientific projects.

I also run an **astrophysics blog**, where I explore topics related to space technology, deep space exploration, and innovative propulsion systems. Check out my work here:
